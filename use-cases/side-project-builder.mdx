A side project lives in the hours nobody else wants. Early mornings, late nights, the odd free Sunday. That is enough time to build, but only if building is what you spend it on. The trap is that the project asks for more than code. It wants launch posts written, customers answered, invoices reconciled, and a read on whether any of it is working. Do all of that yourself and the few hours you have never reach the part you actually care about.

The Solo founder pack is built for that squeeze. It takes the recurring work off your plate and keeps it moving while you are at your day job, asleep, or living the rest of your life, so the time you do get goes straight to building.

## Growth

Getting seen is the work that always slips first, because it is nobody's deadline but yours. This team keeps it going when you cannot.

One agent keeps your newsletter and launch copy going out on time and in your own voice. It gathers your recent posts and product notes, shapes them into a draft with a real through-line, and hands you something to edit rather than a blank page. Nothing reaches a subscriber until you approve it, so the send stays yours.

Another watches the competitors you care about across the open web. Each week it looks for what actually changed, a pricing move, a new feature, a repositioned homepage, and writes up the two or three things that matter with the source linked so you can check it. A rival launch never catches you off guard.

## Analytics

You cannot watch a dashboard when you are only around for a few hours a week. This team reads the numbers for you and tells you what they mean.

One agent hands you a plain-English digest every week. It pulls your funnel and revenue data and leads with what changed, week over week, in words instead of charts. It is honest when a number is down, and it tells you when a swing is probably just noise, so you do not spend a precious evening reacting to randomness.

Another gives you a daily revenue snapshot you can read in seconds. Today's figure, the trend, and the week-over-week delta, so a slow stretch never hides until month end. It reads and reports. It never touches your payments.

## Support

Every question a customer sends is time you did not spend building. This team wins those hours back without letting anyone feel ignored.

It reads incoming tickets as they land, works out what each one is really about and how urgent it is, and drafts a reply in your tone, grounded in your own docs and how you have answered before. Sending on its own stays off until you turn it on, so until then you review and send. When something is a judgment call, a refund, a legal or security question, an angry customer, it escalates with the full context instead of guessing.

## Ops

Invoices and admin pile up quietly, then eat a whole Saturday. This team keeps them from ever getting there.

One agent reconciles your invoices and receipts against your payments and flags anything that does not tie out, a charge with no receipt, a possible double payment, an amount that is off. It never moves money or changes a record to make it balance. It just hands you a short, honest list of what needs your eye.

Another clears the inbox at the end of each week. It archives the newsletters and receipts and notifications you never open, flags the few things that genuinely need you, and leaves a one-minute summary of what it did. It never deletes, so nothing is lost, and Monday starts clean.
