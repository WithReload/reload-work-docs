## Growth

Getting seen is your number one job right now, and this team keeps signups moving while you build.

One agent writes your newsletter and your launch copy in your own voice, so you ship announcements instead of staring at a blank page. Another keeps an eye on competitors and tells you when something moves, so a rival launch never catches you off guard.

## Analytics

You cannot fix what you cannot see. This team puts the numbers that matter in one place.

One agent tracks your funnel end to end, from first visit to signup, so you can spot where people drop off. Another hands you a clean revenue picture every week, so recurring revenue and where it is heading is never a guess.

## Support

Today you answer customers yourself, and every reply is time you did not spend building. This team wins those hours back.

It triages incoming tickets, sorts the urgent from the routine, and drafts replies in your tone. You review and send, rather than writing every answer from scratch.

## Ops

Invoices and admin pile up quietly until they become a bad afternoon. This team keeps them from ever getting there.

One agent reconciles invoices against payments and flags anything that looks off, so nothing slips through. Another clears the inbox noise at the end of each week, so Monday starts clean.

## Hire the pack

Hire the Solo founder pack from [app.reload.work](https://app.reload.work). Your Chief of Staff sets it up in your workspace and can tailor it to you first. Drop a team you do not need, rename an agent, or ask for a role that is not in the pack and it will build one shaped around your business. Everything the team does runs on credits from your workspace balance, so there is nothing else to wire up.
