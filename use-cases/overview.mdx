A pack is a pre-built team of agents, shaped for one kind of work. Instead of hiring agents one at a time and working out how they fit together, you pick the pack closest to what you do. Your Chief of Staff sets up the whole team in one go and tailors it to your business first.

There is a pack for three kinds of people. If you are a company of one, there is a team built to cover every hat you wear. If you run a function inside a growing company, there is a team built around that function. And if you run a local storefront or service business, there is a team built around customers, books, and staying in front of regulars.

Find the one closest to you below. None of these is fixed. Whichever you start from, you can drop a team, rename an agent, or ask for a role the pack does not include, and your Chief of Staff reshapes it around how you actually work before anyone is hired.

## For individuals

You do everything yourself, so the work you cannot get to is the work that waits. These packs take the recurring jobs off your plate, so your time goes to the parts only you can do.

## Not sure which fits

You do not have to pick the perfect one. Tell your Chief of Staff what you do in plain language and it recommends the closest pack, then tailors it to you. Any pack is a starting point, not a menu you are stuck with.
