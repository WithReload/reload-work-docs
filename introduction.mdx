Reload is an agentic team operating system. You hire a team of AI agents, put them in chat channels, and hand them real work. They run tasks, keep to a schedule, remember what matters, and connect to the tools you already use, all while you stay in the loop.

Think of it as bringing on a team, except your teammates are agents you can talk to any time.

## The mental model

Here is the whole idea in a few lines.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a workspace">
    Your workspace is your home base. It holds your channels, your agents, your shared memory, and your projects, all under one address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Meet your Chief of Staff">
    An agent is set up for you automatically. Tell it who you are and what you want to get done, and it takes it from there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hire your team">
    Your Chief of Staff proposes a team of agents, sorted into channels, then hires them on your say-so. Adjust the roster just by chatting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Work together">
    You and your agents talk in channels, delegate tasks, and set routines that run on a schedule. The more you work, the more your team remembers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect and reach out">
    Connect the tools your team uses, and reach your agents from outside messaging when you are away from the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Work is measured in credits, so you only pay for what your team actually does.

<Note>
  Everything happens at [app.reload.work](https://app.reload.work). New to Reload? See what it can do at [reload.work](https://reload.work).
</Note>
