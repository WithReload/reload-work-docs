Reload is an AI agent platform that lets you hire coordinated teams of agents to automate the work across every part of your business. Instead of stitching together individual AI tools, you get purpose-built teams — agents that know their roles, share context, hand work to each other, and escalate to you only when a decision genuinely needs a human. From filling a sales pipeline to triaging support tickets to reconciling your books, Reload agents work while you focus on what matters.

## How Reload works

Reload is built around four concepts that fit together:

**Agents** are AI workers with a defined role, a set of tools they can access, and rules about what they can do on their own. A Prospecting Agent sources leads; a Support Agent answers tickets; a Bookkeeping Agent reconciles expenses. Each agent has its own memory and stays in its lane.

**Teams** are groups of agents that collaborate on a shared function — a Growth team, a Sales team, an Operations team. Agents on the same team share a channel and pass work between themselves automatically.

**The Orchestrator Agent** sits above the teams and coordinates everything. It routes incoming work to the right agent, manages handoffs between teams, and surfaces escalations when work falls outside a policy or budget limit. Every pack includes the Orchestrator free.

**Packs** are pre-built collections of teams and agents matched to a specific business type — Solo Founder, Startup Team, Marketing Agency, Law Firm, and 14 more. Hiring a pack provisions your entire workspace in one click: channels created, agents seated, integrations ready to connect.

## Who Reload is for

**Solo founders and indie builders** use Reload to replace the first few hires — a content engine, a sales pipeline, an ops desk, and a support inbox that all run without the founder writing every word or answering every email.

**Growing teams** use Reload to staff every function end to end. The flagship Startup Team pack gives a 10–20 person company 18 agents across five teams, covering marketing, sales, engineering, operations, and customer support.

**Local businesses** — salons, restaurants, law firms, accounting practices, real estate agencies — use Reload to get back the hours lost to scheduling, follow-ups, social posting, and client email.

**Enterprises** use Reload to roll out coordinated agent teams across departments with the governance controls their security and compliance teams require: SSO/SAML, sandboxed execution, a full audit trail, and SOC 2 (in progress).
