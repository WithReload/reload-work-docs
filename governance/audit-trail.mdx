The Reload audit trail is a complete, tamper-evident record of everything your agents do. Every message, every tool call, every approval decision — all of it is captured with a timestamp, the identity of the agent or person involved, and the full context of what happened. Whether you need to answer a compliance question, investigate an unexpected outcome, or simply verify that a task ran correctly, the audit trail gives you an authoritative, exportable record you can rely on.

## What gets logged

Reload logs every event in an agent's lifecycle. The audit trail captures:

- **Agent messages and outputs** — the full content of every message sent and received by each agent, including prompts, reasoning, and responses
- **Tool calls** — every read and write operation an agent performs on a connected tool, including the exact parameters passed and the result returned
- **Approvals granted or denied** — every escalation raised by an agent, the decision made (approve or deny), who made the decision, and any reason provided
- **Timestamps** — the precise date and time of every event, in UTC
- **Agent identity** — which agent performed each action, so you can filter and trace a full session for any individual agent

Nothing is omitted to save space or simplify the record. If an agent touched something, it's in the log.

## Viewing the audit trail

You can access the audit trail directly from the Reload dashboard:

1. Open the **Audit Trail** section in the main navigation.
2. Use the filter controls to narrow the log by:
   - **Agent** — see all events for a specific agent
   - **Team** — view activity across all agents in a team
   - **Date range** — focus on a specific window of time
   - **Action type** — filter to tool calls, approvals, messages, or a combination
3. Click any log entry to expand it and see the full event detail — including message content, tool parameters, and the agent's stated reasoning at that point.

The filtered view updates in real time as new events are logged, so you can monitor an active agent's session as it runs.

## Exporting audit logs

When you need to share logs with auditors, load them into a SIEM, or archive them externally, Reload supports two export formats:

- **CSV** — suitable for spreadsheet review, audit submissions, and most compliance workflows
- **JSON** — structured format compatible with log management platforms, SIEM tools, and custom pipelines

To export, apply your filters in the audit trail view, then click **Export**. The export includes every event matching your current filter criteria.

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordion title="How long are logs retained?">
  Log retention depends on your plan. Standard plans retain logs for a rolling window. Enterprise plans include configurable retention periods — you can set the retention duration to match your compliance requirements, whether that's 90 days, one year, or longer. Your Reload success manager can help you configure the right policy.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I export logs programmatically?">
  Yes, on Enterprise plans. The log export API lets you pull structured audit data into your own systems on a schedule or in response to events — no manual dashboard exports required. This makes it straightforward to feed Reload audit logs into a SIEM, a data warehouse, or an automated compliance workflow. Reach out to [sales@reload.work](mailto:sales@reload.work) to get access.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are LLM prompt contents logged?">
  Yes. Full message content is logged for every agent interaction — including the prompts sent to the model, the model's responses, and any intermediate reasoning the agent produces. This means you have a complete, readable record of what the agent was thinking and doing at every step, not just a summary of outcomes.
</Accordion>
