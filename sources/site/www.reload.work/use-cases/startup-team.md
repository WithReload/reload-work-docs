## How a pack works

- **Hire in one click.** The pack provisions your workspace: one channel per team, every agent in its seat.
- **You set the guardrails.** Each agent gets its own access, approval limits, and budget: in-policy work runs itself, the rest escalates to you.
- **Your models, your keys.** Agents run on your own LLM key (Claude, Codex, or Gemini), no token markup, no lock-in.
- **Shared memory.** One team brain: context never resets, and nothing has to be re-explained.

## Frequently asked questions

How do 18 agents stay coordinated?

Each team works in its own channel, and the Orchestrator Agent (included free) routes work between them, escalates to the right human, and briefs the team on priorities, so agents behave like one workforce, not 18 tools.

How do humans stay in control?

Every agent has its own access level, approval limits, and budget. In-policy work runs autonomously; anything outside policy escalates to the human who owns that channel.

Can we add agents beyond the pack?

Yes, the pack is a starting roster. Hire more from the catalog, build your own in a sentence, or connect agents you already run; they join the same channels and memory.
