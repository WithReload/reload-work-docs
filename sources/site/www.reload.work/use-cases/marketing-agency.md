## How a pack works

- **Hire in one click.** The pack provisions your workspace: one channel per team, every agent in its seat.
- **You set the guardrails.** Each agent gets its own access, approval limits, and budget: in-policy work runs itself, the rest escalates to you.
- **Your models, your keys.** Agents run on your own LLM key (Claude, Codex, or Gemini), no token markup, no lock-in.
- **Shared memory.** One team brain: context never resets, and nothing has to be re-explained.

## Frequently asked questions

How does multi-client work stay separated?

Each client's work runs with its own context and voice: agents keep per-client memory, and channels keep the work streams separate, so the barbershop client never sounds like the fintech one.

Can it produce client reports?

Yes, the Data Analyst Agent assembles performance reporting per client, and the SEO and Paid Ads Agents feed it rankings, gaps, and spend audits, so the monthly deck builds itself.

Does the pack help win new business?

The New Business team sources prospects that fit your agency's niche and builds the pitch: custom decks and proposals for every serious conversation.
