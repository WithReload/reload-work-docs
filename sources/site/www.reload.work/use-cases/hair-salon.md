## How a pack works

- **Hire in one click.** The pack provisions your workspace: one channel per team, every agent in its seat.
- **You set the guardrails.** Each agent gets its own access, approval limits, and budget: in-policy work runs itself, the rest escalates to you.
- **Your models, your keys.** Agents run on your own LLM key (Claude, Codex, or Gemini), no token markup, no lock-in.
- **Shared memory.** One team brain: context never resets, and nothing has to be re-explained.

## Frequently asked questions

How does the Voice Agent handle booking calls?

It answers the phone, checks the appointment calendar, books or reschedules, and sends confirmations and reminders, transferring to a human for anything it shouldn't decide.

What does the marketing team post?

Before/after posts, seasonal promos, and Instagram-ready creative in your salon's style, drafted for your approval, published on schedule.

How does rebooking work?

The Customer Health Agent watches visit patterns, flags clients overdue for their usual appointment, and drafts the win-back message or offer.
