## Frequently asked questions

Where does deal sourcing look?

The Deal Sourcing Agent scans Crunchbase, PitchBook, YC batches, Product Hunt, Hacker News, and X for startups matching your thesis, and drafts an initial brief on each.

What goes into a diligence pack?

Founder and company background, market sizing, competitive landscape, product analysis, suggested references, and explicit red flags, structured so the partnership reads signal, not raw research.

How does the pack handle passes?

The Deal Follow-up Agent writes thoughtful, personalized passes from the diligence notes, then tracks passed companies and re-flags founders when they hit a milestone worth another look.
