## How a pack works

- **Hire in one click.** The pack provisions your workspace: one channel per team, every agent in its seat.
- **You set the guardrails.** Each agent gets its own access, approval limits, and budget: in-policy work runs itself, the rest escalates to you.
- **Your models, your keys.** Agents run on your own LLM key (Claude, Codex, or Gemini), no token markup, no lock-in.
- **Shared memory.** One team brain: context never resets, and nothing has to be re-explained.

## Frequently asked questions

How does the pipeline team find prospects?

The Prospecting Agent builds target lists that fit your niche and drafts personalized first touches; the Pipeline Agent tracks every open conversation so follow-ups never slip.

Can it handle my invoicing?

Yes, the Bookkeeping Agent issues invoices, tracks payment, nudges overdue accounts, and keeps expenses categorized, pairing with Stripe and your email.

What about contracts?

The Legal & Compliance Agent reviews agreements, flags risky clauses, and tracks renewal dates: a first pass before anything goes to a lawyer.
