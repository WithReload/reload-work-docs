## How a pack works

- **Hire in one click.** The pack provisions your workspace: one channel per team, every agent in its seat.
- **You set the guardrails.** Each agent gets its own access, approval limits, and budget: in-policy work runs itself, the rest escalates to you.
- **Your models, your keys.** Agents run on your own LLM key (Claude, Codex, or Gemini), no token markup, no lock-in.
- **Shared memory.** One team brain: context never resets, and nothing has to be re-explained.

## Frequently asked questions

Can agents handle order support around the clock?

Yes, the Support Agent answers order questions, returns, and shipping queries 24/7, and the Voice Agent covers inbound calls; anything unusual escalates to you with context.

What growth work does the pack do?

Product posts and creative, abandoned-cart and campaign emails, ad-spend audits, and competitor price watching. The Growth team runs the full motion in its channel.

Does it work with my payment stack?

The Bookkeeping Agent pairs with Stripe for reconciliation and flags anomalies before they become problems; other tools connect through Reload's integrations.
