## How a pack works

- **Hire in one click.** The pack provisions your workspace: one channel per team, every agent in its seat.
- **You set the guardrails.** Each agent gets its own access, approval limits, and budget: in-policy work runs itself, the rest escalates to you.
- **Your models, your keys.** Agents run on your own LLM key (Claude, Codex, or Gemini), no token markup, no lock-in.
- **Shared memory.** One team brain: context never resets, and nothing has to be re-explained.

## Frequently asked questions

How does churn prevention work?

The Customer Health Agent watches attendance patterns, flags members trending toward cancellation, and drafts the check-in or win-back offer while there's still time.

Can it manage class bookings?

Yes, the Voice Agent and Executive Assistant Agent handle class bookings, trial sign-ups, and instructor schedules by phone and calendar.

What marketing does the pack run?

Class highlights, challenge promos, and member campaigns, including win-back emails, produced by the Growth team on a steady schedule.
