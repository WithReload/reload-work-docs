## How a pack works

- **Hire in one click.** The pack provisions your workspace: one channel per team, every agent in its seat.
- **You set the guardrails.** Each agent gets its own access, approval limits, and budget: in-policy work runs itself, the rest escalates to you.
- **Your models, your keys.** Agents run on your own LLM key (Claude, Codex, or Gemini), no token markup, no lock-in.
- **Shared memory.** One team brain: context never resets, and nothing has to be re-explained.

## Frequently asked questions

How fast does the pack respond to inquiries?

The Voice Agent answers calls as they come in and the Pipeline Agent logs and routes the lead immediately. Response time stops being a bottleneck.

What listing content does it produce?

Listing descriptions, property creative, social posts, and new-listing alerts emailed to your buyer list. The Listings team runs the full motion per property.

Does it manage viewings?

The Executive Assistant Agent coordinates viewing schedules and reminders with buyers and sellers, so the calendar runs without the back-and-forth.
