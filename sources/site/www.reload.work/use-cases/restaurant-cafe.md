## How a pack works

- **Hire in one click.** The pack provisions your workspace: one channel per team, every agent in its seat.
- **You set the guardrails.** Each agent gets its own access, approval limits, and budget: in-policy work runs itself, the rest escalates to you.
- **Your models, your keys.** Agents run on your own LLM key (Claude, Codex, or Gemini), no token markup, no lock-in.
- **Shared memory.** One team brain: context never resets, and nothing has to be re-explained.

## Frequently asked questions

Can it take reservations during service?

Yes, the Voice Agent answers reservation and takeout calls while the floor is slammed, booking against your capacity and flagging special requests.

What does the marketing team handle?

Daily specials, event promos, and food posts that fill tables, drafted in your voice, scheduled around your service hours.

How does the back office help?

Daily takings reconciled, supplier invoices tracked, and staff scheduling supported. The Bookkeeping and Executive Assistant Agents run it in the background.
