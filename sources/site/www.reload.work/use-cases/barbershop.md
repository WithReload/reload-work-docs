## How a pack works

- **Hire in one click.** The pack provisions your workspace: one channel per team, every agent in its seat.
- **You set the guardrails.** Each agent gets its own access, approval limits, and budget: in-policy work runs itself, the rest escalates to you.
- **Your models, your keys.** Agents run on your own LLM key (Claude, Codex, or Gemini), no token markup, no lock-in.
- **Shared memory.** One team brain: context never resets, and nothing has to be re-explained.

## Frequently asked questions

Can it book appointments while we're cutting?

That's the point: the Voice Agent answers calls, books against each barber's chair schedule, and handles reschedules without anyone putting down the clippers.

What does the local-presence team do?

Fresh-cut posts, local promos, and drafted responses to reviews, keeping the shop top of mind in the neighborhood feed.

How does it bring regulars back?

The Customer Health Agent spots when a regular's overdue for their usual cut and sends a friendly booking nudge, in the shop's voice.
