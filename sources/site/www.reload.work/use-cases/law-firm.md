## How a pack works

- **Hire in one click.** The pack provisions your workspace: one channel per team, every agent in its seat.
- **You set the guardrails.** Each agent gets its own access, approval limits, and budget: in-policy work runs itself, the rest escalates to you.
- **Your models, your keys.** Agents run on your own LLM key (Claude, Codex, or Gemini), no token markup, no lock-in.
- **Shared memory.** One team brain: context never resets, and nothing has to be re-explained.

## Frequently asked questions

How does client intake work?

The Voice Agent answers inbound calls, screens new matters against your practice areas, and routes urgent items to the right lawyer with a summary, no more phone tag.

Can agents review contracts?

The Legal & Compliance Agent does structured first-pass review, flagging risky clauses, missing terms, and deadlines, so a lawyer's time goes to judgment, not scanning.

Is client information protected?

Agents run in isolated sandboxes with access only to what you explicitly grant, actions land in a readable audit trail, and your data is never used to train models.
