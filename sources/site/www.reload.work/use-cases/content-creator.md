## How a pack works

- **Hire in one click.** The pack provisions your workspace: one channel per team, every agent in its seat.
- **You set the guardrails.** Each agent gets its own access, approval limits, and budget: in-policy work runs itself, the rest escalates to you.
- **Your models, your keys.** Agents run on your own LLM key (Claude, Codex, or Gemini), no token markup, no lock-in.
- **Shared memory.** One team brain: context never resets, and nothing has to be re-explained.

## Frequently asked questions

Can the content team match my style?

Yes, agents learn your voice from your existing content and draft platform-native posts, scripts, and newsletters that you approve before anything ships.

How does sponsor tracking work?

The Pipeline Agent treats sponsorships like deals (inbound tracked, follow-ups drafted, deliverables and deadlines watched), and the Bookkeeping Agent keeps creator income and expenses clean.

Which platforms does the pack cover?

Anywhere text and images ship: X, LinkedIn, Instagram captions, TikTok scripts, YouTube descriptions, Discord, and your newsletter. Video editing stays with you.
