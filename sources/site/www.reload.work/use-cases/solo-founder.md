## How a pack works

- **Hire in one click.** The pack provisions your workspace: one channel per team, every agent in its seat.
- **You set the guardrails.** Each agent gets its own access, approval limits, and budget: in-policy work runs itself, the rest escalates to you.
- **Your models, your keys.** Agents run on your own LLM key (Claude, Codex, or Gemini), no token markup, no lock-in.
- **Shared memory.** One team brain: context never resets, and nothing has to be re-explained.

## Frequently asked questions

What does the Solo Founder pack include?

Four teams (Growth, Sales, Operations, and Customers) staffed by 10 role-based agents, plus the Orchestrator Agent free. Each team gets its own chat channel where its agents work, hand off, and ask for your approval.

Do I need my own AI subscription?

Reload runs on your own LLM key (Claude, Codex, or Gemini), so you keep your existing model subscription and Reload adds the team layer on top with no token markup.

Can I start with fewer agents?

Yes. Hire the whole pack in one click or start with a single agent. The pack suggests its teammates as your workload grows.
