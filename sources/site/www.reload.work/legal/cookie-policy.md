## 1.What cookies are

Cookies are small text files a website stores on your device. They let a site remember your actions and preferences, understand how it's used, and, for marketing tags, measure the performance of our ads. “Similar technologies” here means pixels, local storage, and tag scripts that work the same way.

## 2.The categories we use

We group non-essential cookies into two categories you can turn on or off. Strictly necessary cookies always run because the site can't work without them.

- **Strictly necessary**, required for the site to function and to remember your cookie choice, plus the support chat widget. Always on.
- **Analytics**, help us understand how the site is used so we can improve it. Off until you opt in.
- **Marketing**, measure and improve our advertising. Off until you opt in.

## 4.Managing your choices

You set your preferences the first time you visited. You can change them anytime by selecting **Cookie settings** in the site footer, which re-opens the preferences panel. Rejecting a category stops those tags from loading.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings, blocking or deleting them, though some necessary cookies are needed for the site to work. Your choice is stored on **reload.work** and honored by our web app at **app.reload.work**.
