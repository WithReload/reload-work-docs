Eligibility & rules

## The ground rules

Open to anyone ready to put AI agents to work. One entry per person, judged on the work you show.

### Who can enter

- 18 or older, or the age of majority where you live.
- Open worldwide, except where prohibited or restricted by law.
- One person, one entry. Duplicate or fake accounts are removed.
- A valid Reload account, active during the competition window.
- Not a Reload employee, contractor, or a member of their household.
- A reachable contact: winners are reached via their post or email to claim.

### What makes a valid entry

- Your public post is your entry. There's no separate form to fill in.
- One entry per person: your single best video.
- Show your own agents doing real work in Reload, 60–90 seconds long.
- Post it publicly on X or LinkedIn with #ReloadYourWork and tag @reloadwork.
- Keep the post public through the winners announcement.
- The work must be genuinely produced in Reload during Aug 17–30.

Keep it fair and above board. Anything here can remove an entry, or a participant.

### Grounds for disqualification

- Faked, staged, or misleading videos, or work that isn't genuinely yours.
- Content you don't have the rights to, or that infringes someone else's.
- Unlawful, hateful, harassing, deceptive, or NSFW content.
- More than one entry, or duplicate/fake accounts from the same person.
- Posts made private, deleted, or edited so they no longer meet the rules.
- Breaking Reload's Terms of Service or a platform's terms.
- False information, or being unreachable when contacted to claim.

Reload may remove any entry or participant at its sole discretion. Judging and eligibility decisions are final.

No. A Reload account is free and runs on your own LLM key. Everyone who submits a valid entry also gets 1 month of Pro free.

How do I enter?

Sign up for Reload, deploy agents to do real work, record a 60–90 second video of them working, post it on X or LinkedIn with #ReloadYourWork tagging @reloadwork, then submit the link in the form on this page.

One entry per person. Pick your single best video.

Your own AI agents doing real work in Reload, 60–90 seconds, start to finish. Show the actual output, not a mock-up.

In USD to verified winners after we confirm the entry. Winners are responsible for any applicable taxes.
