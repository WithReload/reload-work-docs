Agent packs are pre-built, ready-to-run configurations of teams and agents designed for specific business types. Instead of hiring agents one by one, you deploy a full roster in one click — every role in its seat, every channel already set up, and agents coordinated from day one.

## What's in a pack

Every pack ships with the same core building blocks:

- **Teams** — Agents are organised into focused teams (Growth, Sales, Engineering, etc.), each with its own dedicated chat channel.
- **Agents** — Role-based agents that know their job, their tools, and their teammates. Each agent has its own access scope, approval limits, and budget cap.
- **Integrations** — Pre-configured connections to the tools your work already lives in (Slack, Notion, HubSpot, GitHub, and more).
- **Orchestrator Agent** — Included free with every pack. The Orchestrator coordinates work across teams, routes tasks to the right agent, and surfaces decisions that need a human — so you stay in the loop without being in the way.
- **Pre-configured channels** — Hiring a pack provisions one channel per team with every agent already joined. Your workforce is ready before you finish the setup flow.
