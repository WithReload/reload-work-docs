Every hour you spend prospecting, invoicing, or reading through a contract is an hour you can't bill. For independent professionals, time is the only inventory — and admin drains it faster than any client ever could. The Freelancer / Consultant pack takes the non-billable work off your plate: a pipeline team that finds and tracks prospects, a delivery team that preps meetings and drafts client updates, and a back office that invoices, reconciles, and reads the fine print so you don't have to.

**3 teams · 7 agents · Orchestrator included · Runs on your own LLM key**

## Teams and agents

<Accordion title="Pipeline — #pipeline">
  Finds prospects, builds proposals and custom decks, and tracks every open conversation.

  - **Prospecting Agent** — builds targeted lists that match your niche and drafts personalised first touches
  - **Sales Engineer Agent** — prepares proposals, custom decks, and scoping documents for serious conversations
  - **Pipeline Agent** — tracks every open deal, nudges follow-ups, and keeps the CRM current
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Delivery — #delivery">
  Scheduling, meeting prep, client updates, and deliverable drafting support.

  - **Executive Assistant Agent** — manages your calendar, preps client briefings, and triages incoming email
  - **Content Writer** — drafts status updates, client-facing reports, and deliverable documents
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Back Office — #back-office">
  Invoicing, expenses, contract review, and renewal tracking.

  - **Bookkeeping Agent** — issues invoices, tracks payments, nudges overdue accounts, and categorises expenses
  - **Legal & Compliance Agent** — reviews agreements, flags risky clauses, and tracks contract renewal dates
</Accordion>

Hiring the pack spins up one channel per team with every agent already joined. The **Orchestrator Agent** (included free) coordinates across all three teams and surfaces decisions that need your attention.

## Integrations

The Freelancer / Consultant pack connects to the tools your work already lives in:

- **HubSpot** — CRM, deal tracking, and contact records
- **Gmail** — outreach, client communication, and invoice delivery
- **Google Drive** — proposals, contracts, and client deliverables
- **Notion** — project notes, knowledge base, and SOPs
- **Slack** — team channels and agent notifications
- **Google Calendar** — scheduling, meeting prep, and availability management
- **Stripe** — invoicing, payment tracking, and reconciliation

The pack also connects to any HTTP API or MCP server.

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordion title="How does the pipeline team find prospects?">
  The Prospecting Agent builds target lists that fit your niche from your past client profile and drafts personalised first touches. The Pipeline Agent then tracks every open conversation so follow-ups never slip — even when you're heads-down on a client project.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can it handle invoicing?">
  Yes. The Bookkeeping Agent issues invoices, tracks payment status, nudges overdue accounts, and keeps expenses categorised — all paired with Stripe and your email. You stay in the loop without chasing.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What about contracts?">
  The Legal & Compliance Agent reviews agreements, flags risky clauses, and tracks renewal dates. Think of it as a first pass before anything goes to a lawyer — catching issues early rather than expensively.
</Accordion>
