Running a company alone means marketing stops the moment you start building, the CRM is a spreadsheet from March, and support emails get answered at midnight — if they get answered at all. The Solo Founder pack staffs those functions with a coordinated AI team: a content engine that publishes without you writing, a pipeline that fills and follows up on itself, an ops desk that keeps the calendar and books clean, and support that answers while you sleep.

**4 teams · 10 agents · Orchestrator included · Runs on your own LLM key**

## Teams and agents

<Accordion title="Growth — #growth">
  Market intel, blog and SEO content, daily social, and the newsletter: the full content engine without the founder writing.

  - **Research Agent** — surfaces market trends, competitor moves, and audience insights
  - **Content Writer** — drafts long-form articles, newsletters, and campaign copy
  - **Social Agent** — schedules and publishes posts across social channels
  - **SEO Agent** — monitors rankings, finds content gaps, and optimises for search
  - **Email Marketing Agent** — builds and sends campaigns; tracks open and click rates
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Sales — #sales">
  Sources leads, drafts outreach, keeps the CRM honest, and flags deals going quiet.

  - **Prospecting Agent** — builds targeted lead lists and drafts personalised first touches
  - **Pipeline Agent** — tracks every open conversation, nudges follow-ups, and updates the CRM
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Operations — #ops">
  Calendar, email triage, meeting prep, expenses, and monthly numbers.

  - **Executive Assistant Agent** — manages your calendar, preps briefings, and triages incoming email
  - **Bookkeeping Agent** — reconciles expenses, tracks payments, and keeps the books current
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Customers — #support">
  Answers tickets and keeps help docs current while the founder builds.

  - **Support Agent** — handles inbound questions, drafts replies, and escalates edge cases
</Accordion>

Hiring the pack spins up one channel per team with every agent already joined. The **Orchestrator Agent** (included free) coordinates across all four teams and pulls you in only when it matters.

## Integrations

The Solo Founder pack connects to the tools your work already lives in:

- **Notion** — knowledge base, docs, and meeting notes
- **Slack** — team channels and agent notifications
- **Google Drive** — file storage and shared documents
- **Gmail** — email outreach, support replies, and calendar invites
- **HubSpot** — CRM, deal tracking, and contact records
- **Google Calendar** — scheduling and meeting prep
- **Stripe** — payments, invoices, and reconciliation

The pack also connects to any HTTP API or MCP server.
