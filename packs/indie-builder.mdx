Indie building is a three-front war: PRs pile up with no second reviewer, launch posts never get written because shipping comes first, and user feedback lives spread across ten tabs that you'll get to eventually. The Indie Builder pack covers all three fronts — engineering agents that review PRs and keep tests green, a launch team that turns your commits into build-in-public content, and a users team that answers questions and distils feedback into what to build next.

**3 teams · 8 agents · Orchestrator included · Runs on your own LLM key**

## Teams and agents

<Accordion title="Build — #build">
  First-pass PR review, tests and regressions, and turning ideas into a scoped roadmap.

  - **Code Review Agent** — reviews every PR with inline comments and merge-readiness signals; runs on GitHub
  - **QA Agent** — runs test suites, reproduces reported bugs, flags regressions, and files tickets with full context
  - **Product Manager Agent** — drafts feature scopes, maintains the roadmap in Linear, and synthesises user signals
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Launch — #launch">
  Build-in-public posts, launch threads, changelog articles, and search visibility from day one.

  - **Social Agent** — drafts launch posts and build-in-public threads in your voice; you approve before anything ships
  - **Content Writer** — writes changelog entries, launch blog posts, and documentation
  - **SEO Agent** — surfaces keyword opportunities and keeps your content findable from day one
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Users — #users">
  Handles user questions and turns raw feedback into a prioritised insight stream.

  - **Support Agent** — answers inbound questions, drafts replies, and escalates anything outside its scope
  - **Feedback Agent** — collects and synthesises feedback from email, Slack, and Linear into a prioritised list of what to build next
</Accordion>

Hiring the pack spins up one channel per team with every agent already joined. The **Orchestrator Agent** (included free) coordinates across all three teams and pulls you in only when it matters.

## Integrations

The Indie Builder pack connects to the tools your work already lives in:

- **GitHub** — pull requests, issues, and repository context
- **Linear** — task tracking, sprint planning, and roadmap management
- **Notion** — docs, changelogs, and product notes
- **Slack** — team channels, feedback intake, and agent notifications
- **Google Drive** — file storage and shared documents
- **Gmail** — user email, support replies, and outreach

The pack also connects to any HTTP API or MCP server.

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordion title="Which tools do the engineering agents use?">
  The Code Review and QA Agents pair with GitHub and Linear: first-pass PR review, test runs, regression flags, and tickets filed automatically with full context so you never lose the thread between a bug report and its fix.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can the launch team match my voice?">
  Yes. The Social Agent and Content Writer draft in your voice by learning from your past posts and content. Everything lands in the launch channel for your review — nothing ships until you approve it.
</Accordion>

</Accordion>
