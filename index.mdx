## Get up and running

Go from sign-up to a working AI team in four steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [app.reload.work](https://app.reload.work). No credit card required to start — your account is ready in seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your LLM key">
    Paste in your Anthropic Claude, OpenAI Codex, or Google Gemini API key. Reload encrypts it at rest and never uses it for anything other than running your agents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a pack or hire agents">
    Choose one of the 18+ pre-built packs matched to your business type, or hire individual agents and build your own team from scratch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Put them to work">
    Your Orchestrator Agent spins up, coordinates the team, and starts routing work. In-policy tasks run automatically; anything that needs you gets escalated with full context.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Reload runs entirely on your own LLM keys. Your AI spend goes directly to Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google at their published rates — Reload never marks up tokens or touches your model bill.
</Note>
